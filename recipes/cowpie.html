Cow Pie Recipe

How to make delicious Cow Pies...

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon shortening
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate and shortening together over medium-low heat. Stir until smooth. Remove the mixture from heat and stir in raisins and nuts. Drop cookies by tablespoon onto waxed paper.
  2. Chill and serve.
